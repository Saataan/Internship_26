## Files Included

### Source Code & Testbenches
- `traffic.v` - Verilog module implementing the 4-way traffic light controller with sequential state transitions and clock division.
- `traffic_tb.v` - Testbench used to verify the state timing and emergency preemption functionality.

---

## Tools Used
- Verilog HDL
- Cadence Xcelium

---

## Workflow
1. Developed the RTL design for the traffic light logic in Verilog.
2. Created a testbench to generate input stimuli (including reset trigger).
3. Simulated the design and verified the behavioral output timing waveforms.

---

## Learning Outcomes
- Learned to write synthesizable finite state machine (FSM) logic in Verilog.
- Understood the importance of testbenches in isolating timing bugs early.
