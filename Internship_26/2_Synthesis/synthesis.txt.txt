This folder contains:
- Timing Constraints (.sdc)
- Synthesis Reports (Area, Gates, Timing)

Tools Used:
- Cadence Genus 

Workflow:
1. Loaded the design files and target technology libraries.
2. Applied timing and area constraints via the SDC file.
3. Synthesized and optimized the design into a concrete gate-level netlist.

Learning Outcomes:
- Learned how to integrate physical I/O boundary cells into the design flow.
- Understood how constraints (.sdc) directly dictate gate-sizing and timing paths.
